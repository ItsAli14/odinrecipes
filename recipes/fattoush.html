Fattoush!

Fattoush is a salad variation that originated in my home country of Lebanon. It is made from toasted or fried pieces of Lebanese pitta bread mixed with greens and other vegetables such as tomotoes.

How to make it:

Ingredients!

  • Lettuce
  • Parsley
  • Tomotoes
  • Green Pepper
  • Cucumber
  • Green Onion
  • Radishes

    • Method:

    Firstly, dice the ingredients and follow that up with making the dressing which uses extra virgin olive oil, fresh lemon juice, garlic, mint, sumac and pomegranate molasses,

    Secondly, cut the Lebanese bread into whatever sized pieces you like and fry them with some olive oil,salt and pepper and wait until golden brown

    Finish off by adding all the chopped ingredients to a large bowl. Pour the dressing on topand then mix everything to combine well. But keep the pita bread on the side so that it can be added when served to stay crisp.
