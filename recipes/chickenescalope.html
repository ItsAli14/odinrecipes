Chicken Escalope!

An escalope (which originated in France) is boneless meat pieces that have been thinned out and coated using bread crumbs.

Prep chicken breast in thin fillet cuts; salt and flatten one by one with mallet to reach a uniform thinness. tip: cover breast with plastic wrap before flattening to help contain mess. Put 1/2 the flour in a plate; reserve the rest and add as needed. Crack egg in deep plate and add two tbsp water; scramble. mix bread crumbs with herbs and spices and place half the mixture in a late to coat chicken; add more as needed. Coat chicken fillet in flour; shake off excess. dip fillet in egg mixture coating both sides. coat fillet in bread crumbs making sure entire piece gets covered. continue process until all escalope are coated. Pan fry 2 -3 minutes on each side on medium high heat. tip: for healthier option spray escalope with oil and bake at 350F for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
